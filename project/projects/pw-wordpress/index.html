Prevailing Wins

To provide dragon boating opportunities, competitive and recreational for people of all ages and abilities. To continue introducing youth to the sport of dragon boating by supporting a youth team through coaching and competition.

Recruit new members of all ages by promoting the healthy lifestyle, through paddling and fitness training and acting as team ambassadors by assisting in community events. We value respect for individual abilities, supporting and encouraging each other, and always doing it with humour and camaraderie.
